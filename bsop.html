  • Building a Successful Offshore Partnership
Offshoring shouldnâ€™t be seen as a quick fix to reduce costs. In order for an outsource partnership to be successful, a number of steps need to be taken:
  • Gain support of leadership and management
  • Appoint a committed offshoring champion to drive the process
  • Find a trusted offshore provider to work with your business
  • Ensure that business objectives and outsourcing objectives are clear, understood and that the teams work together to meet them
  • Understand technology requirements to setup your offshore team
  • Identify processes and ensure the provider can help you adapt and streamline them efficiently
  • Commit adequate resources to support the offshoring process
  • Treat the offshore provider as if it were your front office
  • Incorporate the outsource team members into your teams as far as possible
  • Ensure the working conditions at your offshore provider are optimal and that your offshore teams are happy and motivated
