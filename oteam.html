There are many acronyms flying about the outsourcing industry (like BPO!) and a lot of talk about cost savings and efficiency. So it’s really important to remember this is a human business. Our people are our greatest asset and we work hard to ensure that we hire and retain the best.

Ultimately, the team you hire are also an extension of both your team and our wider team as they become a trusted part of your own business. So we would like to introduce a few of our key people.


Sherwyne Anne O. Al Fouzan (General Manager for Human Resources and Administration / President )

Has extensive professional work and leadership experience in human resource management.

She held a variety of positions in Human Resource Management for both large and medium sized local and foreign corporations which contributed enormously to her significant expertise and profound credibility prior joining CCC as an HR Manager on May 2014.

Her core competencies are Recruitment and Selection, Resource and Client Management, Employee Engagement and Relations, Performance Management and Training.

Sher is also a talented freelance makeup artist.


Edgar Sanchez (General Manager for Data Capture)

Joined CCC in 1995 as a Data Encoder and within a span of 1 year was promoted to Quality Control Technician. He became Junior Programmer in 2000 and Production Manager in 2005.

He is very much into volleyball and was one of the coaching staff of the Makati City delegation to the National Capital Region meet and the NCR delegation in the Nationals.


Sunday Doctolero (Information Technology Head)

The Doc started as an agency based data encoder in CCC in 2010 and was absorbed by CCC Philippines in 2013. After a short break he returned as the company’s Systems Support Specialist.

The Doc worked as a High School Computer Instructor for four years and likes playing volleyball and the fun of making computer programs and applications..
