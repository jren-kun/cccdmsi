Shared services groups together a number of key areas of support for business process outsourcing.

CCC services clients across a number of these areas.

Call Centre

A dedicated Call Centre team which can provide both inbound and outbound call centre services.

IT Services

Programmers, Developers, iOS and Android Developers, Website Designers; Help Desk Support; IT Professionals to work on contract or permanent basis

Secretarial

Secretarial tasks which include transcription (audio typing), word-processing, spread-sheets, mail-outs and database management as well as virtual secretarial/PA services

Document Processing

Professionally designed and formatted documents and reports, including PDF conversions

Marketing Assistance

Beautifully prepared sales presentations, promotional brochures, flyers and newsletters

General Requirements

Other general administration that may include: Stationery Ordering, Travel Bookings, Diary Management, Conference Bookings, debt collection and many more functions.

Experts in IT

Perhaps uniquely in the market, our management team, include IT professionals who have run IT consultancies. This means we have a strong understanding of the needs and the issues typically faced in running a business Australia and New Zealand. It enables us to be proactive and strategic in helping you make the most of your offshore team.
